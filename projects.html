Eclipsa

Eclipsa is a 2D cooperative puzzle-platformer for two players, inspired by the classic Fireboy and Watergirl mechanics but reimagined through the dichotomy of light and darkness. Players must simultaneously control two characters, Nivia (night/darkness) and Solenne (day/light), to navigate levels filled with environmental traps and puzzles. The goal of each level is to collect the sun and moon artifacts to trigger the eclipse, restoring cosmic balance.

My Role & Contribution

  • Illustrator: Main menu background illustration.
  • UI Design: User interface and menu design.

Piraña, un gatito extraordinario

Piranha the kitten is different from the other kittens. He has rounder eyes, he can't jump as high, sometimes he wets himself... He just wants to be like the rest of the kittens. One day he meets Mint, a puppy who also has Down syndrome, and who explains to him that they are not strange, they are extraordinary, and that with help and a little more time, they can achieve what any other cat or dog can do, and that they also have a lot to share with their cat or dog friends.

My Role & Contribution

  • Illustrator: At 13 years of age, I brought the characters of the story to life in its first edition. Two years later, I worked on the second edition, digitizing all the characters and filling this beautiful story about Down syndrome with colour.
